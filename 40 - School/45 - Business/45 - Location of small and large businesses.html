Relogic

Relogic is a game developer and publisher, based in Indiana, USA.1 They produce and publish games and sell them online on Steam, a popular game platform. This means that although physically they cannot go to a store and buy games, they can browse online and purchase the game, since they are rated by users. Additionally, the game can be purchased at any time online, since there are no timings for the store being open or not as in physical stores. Since the company is small, and opening a physical store is expensive, Relogic remains online.

Apple

Apple is a technology company, based in Cupertino, California. They create consumer electronics, including phones, computers, and watches.2 They have physical stores, usually located in malls, which are visible to potential customers.3 The shops being in malls means that, since malls get heavy footfall, there are more people potential customers. Apple has both physical stores and online retail, so that customers can purchase products at any time, resulting increased profits since customers can purchase at whatever time suits them. Apple the largest information technology company by revenue4, so they can afford opening numerous physical stores as well.
