These are some pretty good sources for notes and articles of your own. You can even write your own articles as part of your digital garden. Some good articles are in my blog post bookmarks and my blog bookmarks.

Courses and school

Doing programming courses or just taking notes for school help grow your digital garden, and add useful content.

Meta content

Adding guides and information to your digital garden, /about/ your digital garden can help.
