worldbuilding

Karto Nkeia

  • Karto Nkeia rose to great wealth due to an asteroid that crashed on it, which was used to make materials that could modify the properties of atoms on a large and rapid scale.
    • Radio waves emitted by the material when an electric current passes through it alters atoms.
    • Atoms are small, so many waves are needed
    • Due to the nature of the material, it is much easier to destroy objects than rebuild them
    • The material can be used for telekinesis
    • Teleportation is possible, although expensive and dangerous
    • Visors can be developed to show the radio waves, and can mark areas to make it easier to utilize the waves
    • The greater the wave range, the less the power
    • The greater the wave spread, the less the concentrated power
    • The waves alter atoms by 'switching' properties in atoms off or on
      • This essentially is programming the world, implying that it is a simulation
        • Does it matter if they are in a simulation? Life is still the same.
    • The material came from an asteroid, so the supply is limited
      • Exposed contact with the material in military-grade particle editors (which include the material) causes iron deficiency
        • People have turned to consuming blood, rich in iron, in order to mitigate the effects
          • Those with money choose to consume the blood of the rich and famous as a status symbol
          • The blood is also mixed with a drug in order to make it enjoyable to take
          • An entire black market has been developed around this, with people being hired to kidnap the famous, keeping them imprisoned and acquiring blood from them
